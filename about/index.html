About Vincent Wong

Smart Metering | AMI | Smart Water | Utility IoT | NB-IoT

Vincent Wong Yew Hon is a technology and engineering professional with more than 25 years of experience in engineering, product development, embedded systems, IoT technologies, and digital solutions, including over 10 years in Smart Metering and utility technology. His areas of focus include Smart Metering, Advanced Metering Infrastructure (AMI), Smart Water, Utility IoT, NB-IoT connectivity, and utility digital transformation.

He led the end-to-end design, localized Malaysian manufacturing, and execution of advanced utility technology solutions. He writes extensively on smart metering, AMI, IoT, and digital transformation.

Through industry publications, whitepapers, and technical analysis, he shares practical lessons learned from real-world utility deployments across Asia.

His focus is on bridging technology, operations, and business outcomes for utilities undergoing digital transformation, particularly within the ASEAN and Asia-Pacific regions.

Professional Contributions
