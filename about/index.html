About Vincent Wong

Smart Metering | AMI | Smart Water | Utility IoT | NB-IoT

Vincent Wong is a technology and engineering professional with more than 25 years of experience in engineering, product development, embedded systems, IoT technologies, and digital solutions, including over 10 years in Smart Metering and utility technology. His areas of focus include Smart Metering, Advanced Metering Infrastructure (AMI), Smart Water, Utility IoT, NB-IoT connectivity, and utility digital transformation.

He has contributed to the design, deployment, and delivery of utility technology solutions and writes extensively about smart metering architecture, communications technologies, AMI operations, cybersecurity, and digital transformation in the utility sector.

Through industry publications, whitepapers, and technical analysis, he shares practical lessons learned from real-world utility deployments across Asia.

His focus is on bridging technology, operations, and business outcomes for utilities undergoing digital transformation, particularly within the ASEAN and Asia-Pacific regions.
