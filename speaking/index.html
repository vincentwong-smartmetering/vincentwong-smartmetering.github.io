Smart Metering for Asia: Enabling Digital Water Management

Role: Speaker

This presentation explored the role of smart metering technologies in supporting digital water management initiatives across Asia.

Topics included Advanced Metering Infrastructure (AMI), utility digital transformation, operational efficiency, data-driven decision making, and the practical challenges associated with large-scale smart metering deployments.

The session focused on lessons learned from real-world deployments and emerging opportunities for utilities seeking to modernize their water infrastructure.
