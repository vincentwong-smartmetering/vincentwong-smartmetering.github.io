Smart Metering for Asia: Enabling Digital Water Management

Role: Speaker

This technical seminar explored how smart metering technologies and Advanced Metering Infrastructure (AMI) can support digital water management initiatives across Asia.

The presentation covered smart water metering, utility digital transformation, IoT connectivity, operational efficiency, data-driven decision making, and practical lessons learned from real-world deployments.

The session highlighted both the opportunities and challenges faced by utilities implementing smart metering programs at scale.
