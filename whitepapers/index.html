Why Smart Water Metering Projects Fail in ASEAN — And How to Fix Them

A whitepaper examining eight common causes of Smart Water Metering project failure and practical recommendations for utility leaders, AMI project teams, and technology providers.

This whitepaper analyzes the hidden structural, commercial, and technical pitfalls that cause large-scale Advanced Metering Infrastructure (AMI) rollouts to stall across the ASEAN region. Backed by direct field data, it provides utility operators with actionable engineering frameworks to safeguard battery longevity, guarantee telemetry reliability in complex topographies, and achieve maximum NRW (Non-Revenue Water) Reduction while optimizing the Total Cost of Ownership (TCO) of the deployment.
